Pricing FAQs

If you have queries about our pricing, you're in the right place.

It's my first session do I need to pay?

Not at all, your first session is always free.

Do I need to be a member to play competitive?

No, you can compete for us without a Berkshire Royals membership but you'd need a british dodgeball membership to play in one of their leagues.

When does the membership expire?

Memberships expire at the end of the calendar year, we will discount the membership cost partway through the year to account for this.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer for the membership, but sessions and meets are done via Sportas which allows credit card payments.

Is the membership a subscription?

Nope, it's a one off payment not a direct debit.

Do you offer financial assistance?

Yes. We have an inclusion fund that provides support for anyone that would like to play dodgeball but requires financial aid. If you'd like to chat about it, please feel free to reach out to us or speak privately with any committee member. We're here to help and everything will be kept confidential.
